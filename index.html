The Stanford Ventilator

Our goal is to enable dramatically increased production of ventilators in Spring, 2020. We will achieve this speed because our low-cost design has simple parts that can readily be sourced. We are an academic/industry partnership with deep experience in ventilator design and testing. We are seeking partners to scale manufacturing.
